About Grand Master
Affan Kutty

From the busy lanes of Mumbra, Thane, Affan Kutty turned his passion for the classic cube into a spectacular journey of performance-art, records and inspiration.

Having completed over 1,000 cube-mosaic artworks, and honoured with titles such as “Grand Master”, his work spans from elite stage shows to creative installations.
